Jr. Web Developer - Internship

We're looking for enthusiastic individuals who are passionate about web development and eager to learn. As a Junior Developer Intern, you'll have the opportunity to work alongside experienced developers, contributing to the development of our platform while gaining valuable skills and experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the development and maintenance of web applications
  • Collaborate with team members to brainstorm and implement new features
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues as they arise
  • Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in HTML and CSS
  • Proficiency in Javascript, preferred
  • Basic understanding of web development principles
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to work well in a team environment
  • Excellent communication skills

Note: Due to the high volume of applications only qualified applicants will be considered.

Please forward your CV and cover letter to jtimesorg@gmail.com with the subject line "JR DEV".


Social Media Marketing Intern

We're seeking a motivated individual with a passion for social media marketing to join our team as a Social Media Marketing Intern. This internship offers hands-on experience in crafting and implementing social media strategies, as well as the opportunity to enhance our online presence.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and curate engaging content for various social media platforms
  • Assist in developing and executing social media marketing campaigns
  • Monitor and analyze social media metrics to optimize performance
  • Stay updated on industry trends and best practices in social media marketing
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure cohesive brand messaging

Requirements:

  • Demonstrated interest in social media marketing
  • Basic understanding of social media platforms and their respective audiences
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Creative thinking and ability to generate innovative ideas
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment
  • Experience with social media management tools is a plus

Please forward your CV and cover letter to jtimesorg@gmail.com with the subject line "SMM Internship".
